Monday Coffee Expansion Analysis - Key Insights
Project Objective

Analyze customer behavior, sales performance, and city-level market potential to identify the most promising cities for Monday Coffee's future expansion.

Key Findings
1. Coffee Consumer Market Size
Delhi has the largest estimated coffee consumer base, with approximately 7.7 million potential consumers.
Other major markets also show strong coffee demand due to their large populations.
2. Revenue Performance
Pune generated the highest overall revenue among all cities.
Cities with strong revenue performance indicate existing demand and customer engagement.
3. Customer Base Analysis
Jaipur and Delhi recorded the highest customer counts.
A larger customer base suggests stronger market penetration and long-term growth opportunities.
4. Product Performance
Product sales analysis identified the most frequently ordered coffee products in each city.
Understanding local product preferences can support inventory and marketing decisions.
5. Customer Spending Behavior
Average sales per customer varied significantly across cities.
Some cities generated fewer customers but higher spending per customer, indicating premium purchasing behavior.
6. Rent vs Sales Analysis
Cities with lower rent-per-customer ratios offer better profitability potential.
Pune and Jaipur demonstrated particularly favorable cost-to-revenue characteristics.
7. Monthly Growth Trends
Monthly sales growth analysis revealed fluctuations in demand across different cities.
Tracking growth trends helps identify emerging markets and seasonal patterns.
Expansion Recommendations
City 1: Pune

Reasons:

Highest total revenue.
Strong average sales per customer.
Low rent cost per customer.
Excellent balance between demand and operational cost.
City 2: Delhi

Reasons:

Largest estimated coffee consumer market.
High customer count.
Strong revenue generation.
Significant untapped growth potential.
City 3: Jaipur

Reasons:

Highest customer engagement.
Very low rent-per-customer ratio.
Healthy average customer spending.
Cost-effective location for expansion.
Conclusion

Based on sales performance, customer activity, market size, and operational costs, Pune, Delhi, and Jaipur emerge as the strongest candidates for Monday Coffee's future expansion strategy.
